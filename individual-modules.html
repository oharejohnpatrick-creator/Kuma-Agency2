Each 40-hour module is eligible for 40 continuing education units (CEUs) with Yoga Alliance. Upon completing your module — including all required reflections and practicums — you will receive documentation to submit to Yoga Alliance for your CEU record. Yoga Alliance requires 45 hours every three years, so one module nearly fulfills the entire requirement.

Most teachers complete a single module in 8–12 weeks at a pace of approximately 4–6 hours per week. The course is fully self-paced — there are no fixed deadlines — so you can move faster or slower depending on your schedule and depth of engagement.

For most modules, a 200-hour certification or equivalent training is recommended, as the content assumes foundational knowledge of asana, Sanskrit terminology, and basic philosophy.
